Chips Recipe

Home

Description

Air fryer chips.

Ingredients
  • Nasty, stale corn tortillas
  • olive oil or duck fat spray
  • Maldon and/or Lime salt
Steps
  1. slice those terrible factory-made tortillas made with that shitty GMO corn
  2. coat them with spray oil and salt
  3. toss one layer deep on 350 for 3 minutes
  4. flip, repeat
  5. now you have a Mexican omelet breakfast, happy Cinco de Mayo.
