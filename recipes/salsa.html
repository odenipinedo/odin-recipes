Description

Spicy ass salsa roja al molcajete. Makes 3 cups.

Ingredients
  • About 6 plum tomatoes
  • About 8-10 serrano chiles
  • about 5 dried and/or fresh chile de arbol
  • About 4 large cloves of garlic
  • a quarter of a large yellow onion
  • handful of cilantro (grab handfuls of other stuff to add love to the recipe)
  • salt to taste
  • limón (optional)
Steps
  1. throw all the things on a hot ass comal and toast until your eyes water in pain
  2. NOT the cilantro or salt, menso
  3. toast the things until the tomatoes are juicy and chiles are black like your soul
  4. throw ALL the things in the molcajete and grind it like its the backseat of your '94 Corolla
  5. salt is cheap and I know you like to throw it at people, season that shit
  6. for a bit of sour and bitter because I know you are, squeeze that limón like your ex used to do
  7. feed the spicy chile to people you love or hate
