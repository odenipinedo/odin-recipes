Description

Three egg omelet. Don't complicate it.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • unsalted butter, salt, pepper
  • diced chives (optional)

Steps

  1. beat those eggs, you know you want to
  2. heat that pan to screaming hot, you know you want to
  3. you know the heat is right when the butter melts, but does not burn (safe word optional)
  4. fold those eggs in and continuously agitate so the eggs cook uniformly
  5. if you are feeling fancy toss that omelet
  6. add more butter and start to fold the omelet
  7. add salt and chives to taste
